The Bride and Groom

CHARISE

Charise grew up in Niles, IL. She is the baby of the family. Her favorite color red is unsuprisingly the wedding theme. She loves sitcoms and comedy and frequently marathons TV shows such as Friends, Scrubs, 30 rock, and watches stand-up comics like Louie C.K. and Hannibal Burress with Kevin. If time permits, she likes to coupon and learn new skills (her favorite is cutting Kevin's hair). Her natural affinity for computers has led her to a career in information technology.

KEVIN

Kevin grew up in Chicago, IL. He is also the baby of the family. As a result of years practicing Tae Kwon Do, he has a mean high kick. He loves to bake and frequently bakes chocolate chip cookies for Charise family events. He is also the family advisor on technology gadgets, photography, and stock investments. His vast knowledge of technology, photography, and innate leadership skills has allowed him to excel in a management position.

HOW WE MET

It was over a broken laptop. Charise was getting ready for her first year at DePaul when she saw a Circuit City TV commercial advertising free PC checkups for students. The laptop she had planned on using for school crashed everytime she turned it on. She dropped the laptop off and picked it up a few days later after an early morning retail shift. Kevin was the PC technician who worked on her laptop. They've been together ever since (7 years as of August 2013).

THE ENGAGEMENT

On February 14, 2014, Charise's Birthday (also Valentine's Day), Kevin proposed in front of Charise's family after dinner at McCormick & Schmicks, Skokie, IL. Two days prior, Kevin had asked Charise's dad for permission. After dinner, Kevin waited nervously for family to give presents to Charise. His plan was to give his gift (the engagement ring) last. "Unfortunately", everyone forgot their gift at home forcing Kevin to propose upfront. As it turned out, the rest of the family had learned Kevin was going to propose and prepared gifts for Charise related to the engagement/wedding and therefore could not give their gift ahead of Kevin's. Cute naman!
